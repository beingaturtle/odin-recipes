Description:

This is a extremely rare type of cow. Although we're not technically allowed to eat this type of cow, I will teach you how to prepare it anyway.

Ingredients:

  • Miltank
  • A bowl
  • A milk... tank

Directions:

Step 0

You thought that I'd teach you how to prepare a Miltank? Ya right, that's no good.

Instead, I'll teach you how to milk the Miltank.

Step 1

First, take your Miltank and tell her to stay.

Step 2

Second, get your bowl and place it under the Miltank's udders.

Step 3

Third, begin pulling on the udders and aim the milk towards the bowl.

Step 4

Fourth, take the entire bowl of milk and place it inside a milk tank.

Step 5

Ta-da, now you have unlimited milk :]
